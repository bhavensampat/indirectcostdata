Institution:   POLYTECHNIC UNIVERSITY

ONR Indirect Cost Rate Agreement dated 28 June 1996 established fixed
rates with carryforward provisions and fringe benefit rates for 
Polytechnic University for fiscal year ending 30 June 1996.

The purpose of this negotiation agreement is to record agreement on
the carryforward amounts resulting from determination of actual costs
for FY 1996 (FY96).

The rates listed below are based on Polytechnic University proposal
for FY 1996 actual costs dated 6 February 1998, and DCAA Audit 
Report No. 2211-97B10250002 dated 13 May 1998.  These rates have 
been determined in accordance with the provisions of OMB 
Circular A-21.

                      SPECIAL REMARKS

(a)   The actual rates contained in this negotiation agreement are
based on the standard allocation methodologies allowed under OMB
Circular A-21 except as follows:

The FY 96 overhead rate established herein is based on a partial
application of the results of Polytechnic University's space
utilization alternate allocation base survey.  The Government's
agreement to the final overhead rate for FY 96 is non-precedent
setting, and does not imply Government acceptance of the results
of the space utilization alternate allocation base survey in this,
or any other indirect cost rate negotiation.
